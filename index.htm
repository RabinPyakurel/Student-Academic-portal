Our Programs

BCA - Bachelor of Computer Applications

Learn the fundamentals of computer science and programming, preparing you for the tech industry.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Focus: Software Development, IT Solutions
  • Fact: BCA graduates are in high demand in the tech industry.

BBM - Bachelor of Business Management

Develop your leadership, managerial, and entrepreneurial skills with a focus on business operations.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Focus: Business Strategy, Marketing, Finance
  • Fact: BBM grads often secure managerial roles in top companies.

BBA - Bachelor of Business Administration

Get equipped with essential business skills such as marketing, finance, and human resource management.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Focus: Corporate Management, Operations
  • Fact: BBA graduates are the future leaders of global corporations.

BSc. CSIT - Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology

A comprehensive program blending computer science with information technology concepts, ideal for a tech career.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Focus: Programming, Database Management, Networking
  • Fact: BSc. CSIT is one of the most sought-after degrees in IT.
