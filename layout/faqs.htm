Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Student Academic Portal?

The Student Academic Portal is an online platform that provides access to resources such as course registration, academic records, grades, and more.

How do I log in to the portal?

Log in using your student email ID and password. If you encounter issues, use the "Forgot Password" option or contact support.

Can I access the portal on my mobile device?

Yes, the portal is mobile-friendly and can be accessed through a browser.
